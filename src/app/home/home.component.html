The main goal behind developing the web-app ‘Engagement Management Service’ is to provide a more professional and secure means of communication among the staff of a minister by providing a platform where they could arrange the engagements of the ministers and could also share them with staff of other ministers .

Why should you use this webapp?

  • Eases arragements of Engagements
  • Alert Messages
  • Easy updation and cancellation
  • Simple User Interface
